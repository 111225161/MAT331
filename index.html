Grades

Grades will be based on:
(1) In-class quizzes.
(2) Written Homeworks: usually involving mathematical proves and calculation by hands.
(3) Programming homeworks: usually involving writing a short report stating the problem, describing how to solve it, giving the Python/iPython code you used, and table or pictures that illustrate your results.
(4) Projects: Throughout the semester, each of your python codes will contibute to a portion of a final project.
(5) Final exam (similar to quizzes, but longer)

Grades will be calculated using:
(1) 30% Homework. The lowest two grades of your homeworks will be droppbed. However, no late homeworks would be accepted.
(2) 40% Quizes. The lowest one grade of your quizes will be dropped. However, no make-up quizes would be given.
(3) 10% Midterm. There will only be one midterm exam.
(4) 15% Final.
